Passion project

Our grimdark fantasy steampunk world

Synopsis:

The planet is a dying world built by absent gods- a vast artifical system designed to nurture life, powered by the elemental force of Thrym. Life evolved naturally, but it was stabilized by this ancient machinery. Magic emerged as an unintended byproduct, drawn from the very processes meant to keep the world alive.

Now, that system is collapsing. Thrym is running wild. Nature is mutating. Physics is erratic. Time is broken. The planet spirals toward entropy, and its people face extinction or transformation.

Long ago minor gods were placed to watch over the world- but the races of the planet killed them, each gaining twisted powers in the aftermath. Some gods offered gifts freely. Others were consumed violently, leaving behind echoes, curses, or communion.
