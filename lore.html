Thrym

A resource, a fuel, and a source of magic. It seeps from wells hidden in unstable zones, fought over by desperate factions. One group has synthesizes it- unsable and toxic. Another lives sealed in Thrym-fuled domes, surviving in polluted, hyper-industrialized wastelands.

Faction: Steam punk guys

Industrial survivors in domed cities powered by Thrym. Pollution-heavy, airship-bound, clinging to structure in a collapsing world.

Faction: Cultists

Zealots who believe the collapse is holy. Sacrifice is salvation. Magic is sacred death. They hasten the system's end to "broadcast" life to the stars. Monstrous ascendants are born from flesh offerings and eldritch communion.

Faction: Synthetic punks

Mad engineers of corrupted, synthetic Thrym. Their tech is volatile-powerful, but corrupting.

Faction: Rootbound

Nature's twisted immune system. Once caretakers, now genocidal anti-industrial fanatics, spiritually bound to the land and driven to purge artificial life.

The God-Eaters

Slayers of their own god, devourers of divine flesh. Transformed by the act, hunted by the god's spirit an its wrathful echoes. They are eternal nomads being hunted by ghosts of the past.
